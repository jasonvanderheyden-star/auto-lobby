# Whiphand brand and demo assets

Everything for the Whiphand platform and its first offering, Auto Lobby, in one place.

Brand setup: Whiphand is the company, the platform, and the domain (whiphand.com). Auto Lobby is the first offering on it, for lobbying compliance. Intelligence, Grants, and Permits come later on the same platform.

Look and feel: a soft "periwinkle and mist" palette, Space Grotesk and JetBrains Mono type, and the mace mark. The fonts are free from Google Fonts; install them so everything renders correctly.

## What's here

### 01-logo
- `whiphand-mace.svg`: the mace mark, for light backgrounds.
- `whiphand-icon.svg`: the app icon, a mace on a dark tile.

### 02-guidelines
- `Whiphand-Brand.html`: the full brand guide, covering the name, voice, logo, color, type, and product family. Open it in a browser.

### 03-demo (the product walkthrough)
- `Auto-Lobby-Walkthrough.pdf`: the file to send someone. Nine pages, one screen each, opens anywhere.
- `screens/auto-lobby-screen-1 … 9.png`: the same nine screens as images, for email, Slack, or a deck.
- `Demo-Walkthrough.html`: the clickable version. Open it in a browser and use the arrow keys.
- `Auto-Lobby-Walkthrough.pptx`: an editable PowerPoint of the walkthrough.

### 04-deck
- `Whiphand-Pitch-Deck.pptx`: the 14-slide investor deck, editable. The team and ask slides have blanks to fill in.
