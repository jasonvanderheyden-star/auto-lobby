What this is not: a replacement for the once-monthly CEO certification. The senior officer's batched attestation is a legal requirement of the Lobbying Act — this UX is for the attendee (not the certifier), and it serves to raise classifier confidence and reduce false positives by the time the CEO sees the batch.
