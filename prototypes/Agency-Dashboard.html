Mockup context
This is a white-labeled view of Auto Lobby's agency dashboard, shown branded as a fictional firm ("Earnscliffe Compliance"). The agency tier sits above multiple client tenants — each client has its own isolated calendar ingestion, DPOH classification, draft MCRs, and CEO certification flow. The agency user (Sarah, in this mockup) can see portfolio-level health and drill into any client's tenant to act on their behalf.

What's white-labeled: top nav color (--brand-primary), accent color (--brand-accent), agency logo + name, product name in nav.
What's not: the underlying data model, classification logic, audit trail, and per-tenant isolation are identical to the direct-customer experience. The "Powered by Auto Lobby" footer attribution is configurable per agency contract (default on, optional off for premium tier).

See docs/Agency-Motion-Parked.md for the strategic framing and architectural implications. This UI is parked until Phase 4–5 — the architectural decisions to support it (nullable agencyId on Tenant, branding hooks, audit-trail attribution) are non-negotiable from now per CLAUDE.md item #7.
